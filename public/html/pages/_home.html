This idea came about when one of my friends told me about his quest to find the best restroom at his university, UCF. Since then I've come to realize this a common problem that affects all of us. Everyone wants a clean restroom, but finding one isn't always easy.

Hopefully this site will help you find a restroom that works well for you. But remember, this is a community effort. If you find a restroom worth sharing then submit a rating here, and when you find a restroom you like, leave it in a good condition so that others may enjoy it too.
